Hello. My name is Patrick Minero.

I am a coffee connoisseur, craft beer and funky wine lover. I help people, fix and improve things, solve problems, learn new things and keep growing as a professional and a person.

I was born and raised in San Salvador, El Salvador. Despite the bad comments you have heard growing up in El Salvador in the 90â€™s was not such a bad thing. My childhood was very similar to most, watching MTV, listening Alt-Rock and what not. Probably the biggest difference was the language, eating pupusas and playing soccer in the street almost everyday.

Few years go by, I graduated high school and enroll in a university to be a lawyer. This is when my journey began. Violence in San Salvador lead me to fled my home, leave my family and friends and look for new beginnings.

Photo by Hardik Pandya on Unsplash

I moved to the San Francisco Bay Area in 2008, more specifically to Marin County, 10 minutes north of San Francisco. Adjusting was not really difficult for me, I learned to speak English in elementary school, that made it easy to find good job opportunities and have a great time.

Time flew by, 11 years later, my wife and I felt the need for a change. We saved money, worked overtime and by april 2018 we got rid of all our belongings and embarked on a trip without a set route, time frame or final destination. One thing we knew was that we were not going back to California.

Photo by Vitor Monteiro from Unsplash

After 19 countries in 4 continents, we settled down in beautiful Barcelona. Excited for a new beginning, new culture, new people and ready to take on whatever life has ready for me.
